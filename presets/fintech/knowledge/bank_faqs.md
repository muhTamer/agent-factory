# Bank FAQs

## Account & Access
**How do I open an account?**  
You can open an account directly from the mobile app using your national ID or passport.

**I forgot my PIN. What should I do?**  
You can reset your PIN in the app under *Settings → Security → Reset PIN*.

## Payments & Transfers
**Do you support international transfers?**  
Yes, international transfers are supported to most countries. Fees and delivery times vary by destination.

**Are card payments instant?**  
Yes, card payments are processed instantly in most cases.

## Support
**When is customer support available?**  
Customer support is available 24/7 via in-app chat.
